# Client request — email

**From:** Camille Roy, COO @ Nova Studio (a 25-person agency)
**To:** Product team
**Subject:** We need a simple tool to track our team's tasks

---

Hi,

We're growing fast and we're losing track. Right now everyone manages their tasks in their own corner — one in Notion, one on sticky notes, two in a shared spreadsheet nobody keeps up to date. The result: at Monday's standup, nobody really knows where we stand, and I spend my time asking "is that done or not?".

What we'd like, without the bloat:

- One single place where everyone sees their tasks and the team's
- The ability to move a task from one stage to the next (to do → in progress → done)
- **And above all:** that I, as COO, can see at a glance where we stand overall. How many tasks in progress, how many finished this week, are we speeding up or slowing down.

We've already tried heavy tools like Jira — too heavy for us, the team just doesn't open them. I want something people actually use.

Reasonable budget, and we need it fast — ideally a first version within 6 weeks.

Thanks,
Camille
